# Privacy Policy

**Plextura Suite**
**Effective Date: March 8, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how DiAhman Contracting, LLC ("we," "us," or
"our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Plextura Suite
software, Plextura Cloud services, and related websites (collectively, the
"Services").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

When you create a Plextura Account through the portal, we collect:

- Email address
- Password (stored as a bcrypt hash; we never store plaintext passwords)
- Full name (if provided)
- Organization name (if provided)

### 1.2 Instance Registration

When a Plextura Suite module connects to Plextura Cloud, we collect:

- Module name and version
- Server hostname
- Instance identifier (UUID)
- License tier and entitlement status

### 1.3 Licensing and Activation

When software is activated or performs periodic check-ins, we collect:

- License key identifier
- Machine fingerprint (a one-way hash derived from hardware characteristics;
  we cannot reverse this to identify specific hardware)
- Activation timestamp
- Check-in timestamp and status

### 1.4 Telemetry (Opt-In Only)

If you enable anonymous telemetry, we collect aggregated, non-identifying
usage statistics such as device counts, feature usage patterns, and module
configuration. Telemetry is:

- **Disabled by default** — you must explicitly opt in
- **Anonymous** — no personally identifiable information is included
- **Aggregated** — individual data points are not stored or analyzed in
  isolation

You can disable telemetry at any time in your module's settings.

### 1.5 Website Access Logs

Our web servers collect standard access logs:

- IP address
- Timestamp
- User agent string
- Requested URL and response code

These logs are retained for security and operational purposes only.

### 1.6 Transactional Email

When we send you email (registration confirmation, password reset, trial
notifications), we record:

- Email address
- Email type and send timestamp
- Delivery status

We do not track email opens or link clicks.

---

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

### 2.1 Your Business Data

Network topology data, device information, monitoring metrics, client
records, agent configurations, and all other data entered into or collected
by Plextura Suite modules remains on your infrastructure. We do not have
access to this data unless you explicitly grant it for support purposes.

### 2.2 No Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
for their marketing purposes. We do not use advertising networks or
third-party analytics services.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

a. Provide and maintain the Services (account management, licensing,
   software distribution);
b. Verify license compliance and prevent unauthorized use;
c. Send transactional communications (registration confirmation, password
   reset, trial expiration reminders);
d. Improve the Services based on aggregated, anonymous telemetry (if opted
   in);
e. Ensure security and detect abuse (rate limiting, clone detection);
f. Comply with legal obligations.

---

## 4. Marketing Communications

### 4.1 Opt-In Only

We only send marketing communications (product announcements, newsletters)
if you explicitly opt in during registration or in your account settings.
Marketing opt-in is separate from account creation and is unchecked by
default.

### 4.2 Unsubscribe

Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe link. You can also manage your
marketing preferences in your Plextura Account settings.

### 4.3 Transactional vs. Marketing

Transactional emails (registration confirmation, password reset, trial
notifications, security alerts) are not marketing and do not require opt-in.
These are necessary for the operation of your account.

---

## 5. Data Retention

| Data | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| Account information | Until you delete your account |
| Instance registration | Until instance is deregistered or account deleted |
| Activation records | Duration of license + 90 days |
| Access logs | 90 days |
| Telemetry data | Aggregated indefinitely; raw data purged after 12 months |
| Transactional email records | 12 months |

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 Access

You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you by contacting
us at the address below.

### 6.2 Correction

You may update your account information at any time through the Plextura
portal.

### 6.3 Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated personal data.
Upon deletion:

- Your Plextura Account and profile information will be permanently removed
- Active licenses will be deactivated
- Instance registrations will be removed
- We may retain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be linked back to you

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

### 6.4 Data Export

You may request an export of your personal data in a machine-readable format.

### 6.5 Objection and Restriction

You may object to or request restriction of certain processing activities.
Contact us to discuss your specific situation.

### 6.6 GDPR (European Economic Area)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights
under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to
lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Our legal basis
for processing is:

- **Contract performance** — account management, licensing, service delivery
- **Legitimate interest** — security, abuse prevention, service improvement
- **Consent** — marketing communications, telemetry

---

## 7. Data Security

We protect your data using:

- Encrypted connections (TLS) for all data in transit
- Bcrypt password hashing
- Ed25519 signed activation responses to prevent tampering
- Rate limiting and abuse detection
- Access controls on server infrastructure

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee
absolute security, but we apply industry-standard practices.

## 8. Cookies

### 8.1 Portal Cookies

The Plextura portal uses essential cookies for:

- Session management (authentication tokens)
- CSRF protection

These are strictly necessary for the portal to function and do not require
consent.

### 8.2 No Tracking Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies on
any of our websites or services.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not
knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that
we have collected personal information from a child, we will delete it
promptly.

outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and
processed in the United States. By using the Services, you consent to this
transfer.
